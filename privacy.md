This server is self-hosted and privately operated. Your data stays on the server operator's hardware — it never touches third-party services unless stated below.

## What we store

- **Account** — Username, email, and hashed password.
- **Photos & files** — Whatever you upload, on the server's storage.
- **Passwords** — End-to-end encrypted. Only you can decrypt.
- **App data** — Bookmarks, notes, preferences, etc.
- **Auth logs** — Login attempts, retained 30 days.

## What we don't do

- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
- No advertising or data selling
- No browsing history monitoring

## Security

- TLS encryption on all connections
- Automated encrypted backups
- SSO with rate-limited logins and brute-force protection

## Your rights

- View, download, export, or delete all your data anytime
- Contact the server operator for any questions or concerns
